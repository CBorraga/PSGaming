Call of Duty: Modern Warfare (2019)

A little bit about Call of Duty: Modern Warfare (2019):

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare is a first-person shooter video game developed by Infinity Ward for the Xbox One, PlayStation 4 and Windows. It is a reboot of the original Modern Warfare trilogy. Call of Duty: Modern Warfare is the sixteenth game in the Call of Duty franchise, the fourth game in the Modern Warfare series, and Infinity Ward's second to be developed in a three-year development cycle. Announced on May 30th, 2019, the game was released on October 25th, 2019.


5 out of 5 stars!


Definitely a must have! It's a first person shooter that could get you into the FPS genre! Try for free here.

My review:

This is a lasting series that most familiar with consoles/gaming pc's know about. This new Modern Warfare is easy for everyone to get to know, and understand the concepts of. They have many different playstyles, but honestly the only bad thing I have to say about this one is that once you become good at it, you'll end up facing literally the people who never get off the game. Which is great, depending on if you want to get that good or not. Sometimes it is a chore to just sit there and play the hardest you can for prolonged amounts of time.

This Modern Warfare has a few game modes that are pretty neat. Warzone is the mode that is trending right now, both on twitch, and the game in general. Warzone is just a free game mode that puts you up against 149 other players. (150 including you.) This mode features Solos, Duos, Trios, and Squads(4).

They also have Campaign, Multiplayer, and Spec Ops. Campaign has a thrilling storyline. Multiplayer pits you against anywhere of 1 vs 6, to 40 vs 40. Multiplayer is the best game mode in my opinion, especially if you're aiming to get better. You can challenge anyone with anything, if you're a rushing type, or a sit back and snipe type. It's your playground, have fun with it!
